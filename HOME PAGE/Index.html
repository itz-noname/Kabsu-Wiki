KABSU Wiki

KABSU Wiki is an informational platform created to serve the Cavite State University (CvSU) community. Our primary purpose is to document, preserve, and share knowledge about the university — including its history, colleges, organizations, events, and student life.

We are a student-led initiative built on the belief that information should be open, accurate, and accessible to everyone. Whether you are a freshman finding your footing, a faculty member looking for resources, or an alumnus revisiting your alma mater, KABSU Wiki is here for you.

While we strive to provide accurate and up-to-date information, please note that KABSU Wiki holds no warranties or representations regarding the completeness, accuracy, or reliability of any content on the site. We encourage our readers to verify critical information through official university channels.
