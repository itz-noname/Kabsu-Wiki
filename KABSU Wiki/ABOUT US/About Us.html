About Us

Learn who we are, what we stand for, and where we're headed.

Mission, Vision, Goals & Objectives
What We Do
Mission
To create and maintain a free, open, and community-driven informational platform that documents the people, programs, organizations, history, and culture of Cavite State University, empowering every member of the CvSU community with accurate, accessible, and well-preserved knowledge.
Where We're Going
Vision
To become the most trusted and comprehensive online reference for the CvSU community. A living digital archive where stories, achievements, and knowledge are preserved for current students, faculty, alumni, and future generations alike, fostering pride and unity across the university.
What We Aim For
Goals
01

Community Engagement — Cultivate an active contributor base of students and faculty who help keep the wiki current and growing.

02

Accessibility — Make all information freely and easily available to anyone in the CvSU community, regardless of technical background.

03

Platform Growth — Expand KABSU Wiki into a recognized digital hub that complements official CvSU communications and resources.

How We Get There
Objectives
Publish and maintain dedicated pages for every college, department, and recognized student organization within CvSU.
Implement a structured editorial process with designated editors and a clear content review workflow.
Partner with CvSU student organizations to source firsthand information about their history and activities.
Grow the contributor team by recruiting passionate CvSU students each academic year.
