University Seal
The official emblem and symbol of Cavite State University

The University Seal is the official emblem of Cavite State University (CvSU), a public state university located in Indang, Cavite, Philippines. The seal serves as the primary symbol of the institution's identity, authority, and academic tradition, appearing on official documents, diplomas, academic regalia, and institutional publications. Each element within the seal carries distinct symbolic meaning rooted in the university's history, mandate, and values.

Overview

The University Seal of Cavite State University shall be a square with one-third of one of its angles cut to form its base. It shall assume the shape of a space capsule. The dominant colors of the University seal are Green and Gold. The words “Cavite State University”, “Cavite”, “Philippines”, “1906” are written in gold against a dark green background around the periphery of the seal.

The seal is governed by the Office of the University President and its use is strictly regulated through official university policy. Unauthorized reproduction or use of the seal is prohibited under Republic Act No. 8293, the Intellectual Property Code of the Philippines. The current design of the seal has been in official use since the university was granted university status through Republic Act No. 8468 in 1997.

The University Seal should not be confused with the CvSU Hymn or the university logo used in digital media. The seal specifically refers to the formal heraldic emblem used for official documentation and institutional ceremonies.

Central Elements Symbolism

The CvSU seal is composed of four important interactive elements that represent the vision, thrusts, and the programs of the University:

Element Symbolic Meaning
Book with Torch At the center of the seal symbolizes knowledge and wisdom. It also represents education, the humanities and other social science programs of the University.
Coffee Twig with Ripe Berries At the right hand side of the book with torch symbolizes the major agricultural thrusts of the Province of Cavite. It also represents the agriculture and related programs of the University.
Gear At the bottom of the book with torch symbolizes engineering and technology programs of the University.
Atomic Structure At the left hand side of the book with torch represents the science programs, and more importantly, the scientific research undertakings of the University.
Flame of the Torch Designed as “CvSU” which is the adopted acronym of the Cavite State University. The flame symbolizes the enduring and everlasting flame that will guide the University in its search for truth.
