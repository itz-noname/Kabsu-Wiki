CvSU Hymn
The official anthem of Cavite State University

The CvSU Hymn is the official anthem of Cavite State University (CvSU), a public state university in Indang, Cavite, Philippines. The hymn is sung during official university ceremonies, convocations, and significant institutional events, serving as a musical expression of the university's identity, values, and aspirations for its students, faculty, and the communities it serves.

Overview

Like most Philippine state universities, CvSU maintains an official hymn that encapsulates the institution's founding purpose, core values — Truth, Excellence, Service, and Integrity — and its commitment to the Caviteño community. The hymn is traditionally performed during the university's founding anniversary, commencement exercises, and other major academic milestones.

The melody and lyrics reflect the university's agricultural and technological roots, celebrating its growth from a provincial farm school into a nationally recognized university with multiple campuses across the province of Cavite. Students and alumni often regard the hymn as a symbol of Iskolar ng Bayan pride.

Notes on the First Verse

The opening verse traditionally introduces the university's name and geographic identity within the province of Cavite. It often references the institution's mission to educate and uplift the community through higher learning and technological advancement.

Chorus / Refrain

The refrain typically serves as the emotional and thematic core of the hymn — a rallying call that unites all members of the CvSU community under shared values. It is the most widely recognized portion of the piece and is most frequently sung during ceremonies.

Chorus 2 (Ending)

The chorus 2 ending of the Cavite State University (CvSU) Hymn is all about empowerment, community service, and the lifelong legacy of the institution.

Significance & Traditions

The CvSU Hymn is performed at the start or close of major ceremonies including: commencement exercises, college days, university founding anniversary celebrations, and formal convocations. Faculty, students, and guests are expected to stand during its performance as a sign of respect for the institution.

For many alumni, the hymn evokes a strong sense of nostalgia and pride — a musical thread connecting generations of CvSU graduates. It is considered one of the key symbols of institutional identity alongside the University Seal and the Historical Landmark.
